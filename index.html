How a project runs

One campaign week, every week.

The same rhythm behind every brand I manage, condensed into four days.

Day 1 — Discover

Audit & strategy

Audience, competitors, and past performance, distilled into a content pillars doc.

Day 2 — Plan

Calendar & briefs

A month of posts mapped to platform, format, and goal — no guessing what goes out next.

Day 3 — Create

Shoot & write

Scripts, captions, and either shot content or clear briefs handed to your in-house team.

Day 4 — Grow

Post, reply, report

Publishing, community replies, and a monthly report tied back to the original goals.
